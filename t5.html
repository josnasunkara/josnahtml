Angela's Recipe for the Best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • ¾ cup warm milk
  • 2¼ teasppons yeast
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • ¼ cup better
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • ⅔ cup dark brown sugar
  • 1½ tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • ¼ cup better

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast ,sugar,eggs.
  2. Melt the butter and add to mixture
  3. Add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough.
  4. Knead the dough for 10 minuities.
  5. Transfer the dough into a largebowl and cover with plastic wrap.Leave it somewhwere to rise for 2 hours.
